Our Purpose

Vision: To be the regional standard for digital laboratory management — where every school operates with full transparency, efficiency, and accountability through the power of technology.

Mission: To provide a real-time inventory tracking system that safeguards school assets, simplifies the borrowing and returning process, and ensures every student has equal access to the tools they need.

Why We Made This Website

The JCSLC Borrowing and Inventory System was born out of a necessity observed during our time last school year. Traditional manual logging—using pen and paper—often leads to lost records, inaccurate quantity counts, and a lack of accountability when equipment is damaged or missing. We realized that as ICT students, we had the tools to solve this problem.

This website serves as our capstone research project, designed specifically to digitize the borrowing and returning process. By digitizing the inventory, we provide teachers with a bird's-eye view of all assets, from microscopes to beakers, ensuring that every piece of equipment is accounted for. This project represents our commitment to improving our school's infrastructure, fostering a more organized environment for future students at JCSLC.
